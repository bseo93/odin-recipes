Guacamole

Description

This easy guacamole recipe is delicious and a great crowd pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados -peeled, pitted and mashed.
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in cayeene pepper. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
